Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

HouseholdMath provides free calculators to help people work through everyday personal-finance decisions. This page explains what happens to your information when you use this site.

What we don't collect

Every calculator on this site runs entirely in your browser. The numbers you enter, income, expenses, balances, debts, goals, are never sent to a server, stored in a database, or seen by anyone. There is no account, no login, and no form that submits your data anywhere.

Data shared between calculators

To save you re-typing, some calculators can carry figures you entered into another calculator during the same browsing session, for example passing your essential expenses from the budget calculator to the emergency fund calculator. This uses your browser's own temporary session storage. It stays on your device, is never transmitted anywhere, and is cleared automatically when you close the tab. You are always asked before any figures are loaded.

What we do collect

This site uses standard web analytics and, once approved, Google AdSense to display ads. These services may use cookies or similar technology to:

  • Understand general traffic patterns, like how many people visit and which pages they view
  • Show relevant ads based on general browsing behavior

This information is aggregate and anonymized. It is not tied to the specific numbers you enter into any calculator, since those never leave your device in the first place.

Google AdSense and cookies

If this site displays ads, Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-party links

Some pages link to external resources, such as Google's Ads Settings above or the public data sources cited in a calculator's methodology. This policy doesn't cover those sites. Check their own privacy policies before providing any information to them.

Not financial advice

The calculators produce general estimates for planning purposes only. They are not professional financial, tax, or legal advice, and do not recommend any account, product, lender, or investment. Consult a qualified advisor before making individual financial decisions.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update date at the top of this page will reflect that. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the contact listed on the site's GitHub repository.
